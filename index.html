Admissions

Admission Guidelines

We welcome students from all backgrounds who are eager to learn and grow. The admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward.

Required Documents

  • Original Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Leaving Certificate (TC)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Proof of Residence
  • Report Card of the previous year

Academics

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning. We follow a globally recognized syllabus integrated with innovative teaching methodologies.

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environmental Science with state-of-the-art laboratories.

Mathematics

Advanced problem solving, logical reasoning, and practical applications.

Humanities

History, Geography, Political Science, and Economics.

Languages

English, Hindi, Malayalam, and optionally Arabic or French.

Technology

Computer Science, Coding, Robotics, and Digital Literacy.

Arts & Design

Fine Arts, Music, Dance, and Performing Arts.
